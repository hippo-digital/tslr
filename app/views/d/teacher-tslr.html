About this scheme

From {{ globals.policy_criteria.start_date }}, teachers can claim back any student loan payments made through their wages between {{globals.tax_year.start_date}} and {{globals.tax_year.end_date}} (the last tax year).

The scheme is for teachers in certain subjects and is only running in certain areas of England – areas that have fewer teachers in these subjects at the moment.

The money you get back will be in one lump sum, and you will not have to pay tax or National Insurance on it.
