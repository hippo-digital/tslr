Make a claim

Before you start

You will need:

  • the total amount of student loan repayments made through your school wages in the last tax year – you can find this on your payslips
  • sign in details for ‘Verify’, the government’s secure way of proving who you are

If you do not have a Verify account, you will also need:

  • your passport or photocard driving licence
  • your teacher reference number – you can get this from your school, the certificate you got when you qualified as a teacher or from the teacher qualifications helpdesk
  • your National Insurance number

You've worked at more than one school

If you've worked at more than one school since {{ globals.tax_year.start_date }}, apply for each school separately.

Other ways to apply

If you cannot apply online, you can [apply by post]. The address to send your application to is at the end of the form.
